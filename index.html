Rules

Divide the current number by 2, 3, or 4 on your turn. Score a point if your division results in a whole number. No points for decimal results. Players take turns until the number becomes too small to continue. The player with the most points wins.

Tips

  • Check if your division will give a whole number before playing. Plan your moves to force your opponent into decimal results. Prime numbers multiplied by 2, 3, or 4 offer good strategic options.
  • Watch for patterns in the computer's strategy. Even numbers often provide more whole-number division possibilities than odd numbers.
